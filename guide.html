Travel Guide

Visas • Luggage • Culture • Lifehacks

Visas & Documents

Make sure your passport is valid for at least 6 months after your trip.
Check visa requirements of your destination country and apply in advance.
Keep copies of documents (passport, tickets, insurance) in a separate place.

Language Tips

Pack light — bring only essentials, you can buy many things locally.
Always keep medications, documents, valuables and a change of clothes in your carry-on.
Check baggage weight limits to avoid extra charges.

Local Culture

Learn about local customs and traditions to avoid awkward situations.
Study a few basic phrases in the local language to connect with people.
Respect dress codes, especially at religious or cultural sites.

Travel Lifehacks

Download offline maps and translation apps before traveling.
Always have some local currency for small purchases and emergencies.
Bring a universal adapter and a power bank for your devices.

Tip of the Day

Roll your clothes instead of folding them — this saves space in your suitcase and keeps them less wrinkled!
